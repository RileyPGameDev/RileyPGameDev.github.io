Dambuster Studio Experience

Documenting my experience working with Dambuster Studios via IntoGames.

About the Project

In my first year of college, I was one of the very few students hand picked to participate in work experience with Dambuster Studios, this interaction was done via IntoGames.

I was put into a team with 5 other students, we had our roles that we specialised in. I was one of two programmers, we also had 2 artists and 2 3D modelers, and we were assigned a mentor to help guide us if we ever needed it.

I was elected by my team as the project manager, this came with responsibilities such as:

  • Leading daily stand ups
  • Making dificult decisions
  • Checking up on my team and helping with any issues that came up
  • Adjusting to my team members work style and flows
  • Reporting back to our mentor
    •

    Just to name a few.

    We had only 5 days to get this asset done, however, the first day was about idea generation, and the last day was presenting. So we only had 3 days to get the asset produced.
